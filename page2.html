Events

National Service Scheme volunteers participate in many social events throughout the year.
Gyanmalini

Gyanmalini is an initiative of NSS Cell, Tezpur University. Under this scheme, the Volunteers visit their allotted schools nearby, headed by a group of seniors every weekend providing educational, counselling and support to the ...

Blood Donation Camp

Tezpur University organized Mega Blood Donation Camp on 19/02/2022, in collaboration with Indian Red Cross Society, NSS and NCC with the assistance of Tezpur Medical college in which 76, NSS and NCC volunteers successfully donated blood out of total 106 registered volunteers.

Daan Utsav

NSS Volunteers of Tezpur University organised Daan Utsav under Gyanmalini on 15th May, 2022 within the University premises to inculcate the spirit of charity.

Yoga Camp

The 8th edition of the International Yoga Day was celebrated at a grand scale in Tezpur University. Being one of the 75 iconic destinations where Yoga mahotsav was observed, NSS Cell, Tezpur University organised mass Yoga demonstration at the University premises to commemorate the occasion.

Nasha Mukta Bharat Abhiyaan

Poster making competition organized by NSS,Tezpur University on the theme "Awareness on Substance abuse" as a part of the Nasha Mukt Bharat Abhiyaan. 121 Volunteers actively participated and many creative posters were made by them.

International Women's Day

Poster Making and Slogan Writing Competition held on 9th March in the 1 Week long campaign in recognition of International Women's Day 2022. Students were given two topics: Women Empowerment and Women's Health and Hygiene .

World Bicycle Day

Bicycle Rally organised by NSS Cell, Tezpur University on the occasion of World Bicycle Day on 3rd June, 2022. A great participation was observed from the volunteers to spread the message of a clean and green environment.

International Yoga Day

The 8th edition of the International Yoga Day was celebrated at a grand scale in Tezpur University. Being one of the 75 iconic destinations where Yoga mahotsav was observed, NSS Cell, Tezpur University organised mass Yoga demonstration at the University premises to commemorate the occasion.

Cyber Peace Foundation

NSS Volunteers from Tezpur University attended a webinar organized by Ministry of Youth Affairs and sports on "Data Privacy and Social Media Safety" conducted by Cyber Peace Foundation on 16th July, 2022.

Har Ghar Tiranga' campaign

NSS Cell Tezpur University organised Tiranga Cycle Rally to create awareness about the 'Har Ghar Tiranga' campaign, under the aegis of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav. The Cycle Rally was organised to encourage people to bring Tiranga home and hoist it to mark the 75 years of Indian Independence..

National Nutrition Week

On the occasion of National Nutrition Week, NSS volunteers from Tezpur University participated in a poster making competition organised within the campus to generate awareness related to healthy and balanced nutrition.

World Suicide Prevention Day

On the occasion of World Suicide Prevention Day, NSS Cell Tezpur University set an example of creating hope through action by organising a street play event in collaboration with Darrang College and Youth Wellness Hub, LGBRIMH.

Swacch Bharat Abhiyan

NSS Volunteers of Tezpur University cleaning University Campus as a part of Swacch Bharat Abhiyan.

Annual Day Celebration

Annual Day Celebration at Tezpur University, marked with cultural performance and award ceremony for the senior volunteers of National Service Scheme.

Pariksha Pe Charcha

NSS volunteers of TEZPUR UNIVERSITY attending Pariksha Pe Charcha (An interactive session where Prime Minister Narendra Modi interacts with board exam students and answers their queries on exam stress and related issues) held on 1st April 2021.
