Programme

Gyanmalini
Education


“Gyanmalini” is an initiative of NSS Cell, Tezpur University. Under this scheme, the Volunteers visit their allotted schools nearby, headed by a group of seniors every weekend providing educational, counselling and support to the Children of Nearby schools. Currently we have 3 adopted schools, with an overall strength of 288 school students.

NSS cell TU to adopt nearby Government School and gave them free skilful session along with tuition classes taken by our volunteers every weekend and from 4 years it runs successfully
